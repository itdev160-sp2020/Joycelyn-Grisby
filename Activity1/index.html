Traveling Toronto

There are so many awesome things I love about Toronto, Ontario. I first traveled to Toronto when I was 1 years old.
My parents first started traveling to Toronto in the late 1970's, back when you didn't need a passport.
Growing we spent 2 weeks there in the month of July every year. My father isn't big on planes, so we drove. It became a second home and it still is. There is so much to do especially in the summer.
Let's talk about some of my favorite thingsand places.
